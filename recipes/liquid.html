Liquid

The best choco-banana protein smoothie for breakfast or between meals

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 minute
  • Serves: 1-2

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup water or milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate protein powder
  • 1/2 cup oats

Directions:

  1. Break banana into pieces and put into blender along with oats, protein powder, and milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and enjoy!
