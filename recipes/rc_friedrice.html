Rice Cooker Fried Rice

The most hands-off cooked meal, perfect for in-office lunch

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1-4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 potato peeled (used a smaller potato so that it can be fully cooked through)
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced Chinese sausage
  • 1 cup onions
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons oil
  • salt

Directions:

  1. Rinse rice in cold water and gently swirl the grains. Then pour the water out. Repeat 3 times.
  2. Pour the rinsed rice into the rice cooker.
  3. Add a peeled potato to the center, then add carrots, sausage, onions, and green peas to the rice cooker.
  4. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and oil.
  5. Pour in 1 cup of water, and close the lid.
  6. Select “white rice” (if option exists). It's usually about 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on your model.
  7. When the timer goes off, lift the lid and break the potato using a spoon. Then gently mix everything together. (Be careful not to scratch the bottom of your rice cooker)
  8. Season with salt to taste. Serve and enjoy!
